# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides insights into employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and job roles. It helps HR teams identify trends and improve employee retention strategies.

## ✨ Features

- Employee Attrition Analysis
- Department Filter
- Gender-wise Analysis
- Education Field Distribution
- Salary Slab Analysis
- Age Group Analysis
- Job Role Analysis
- Years at Company Analysis

## 📊 Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|----------|----------|
| Total Employees | 961 |
| Attrition Rate | 100% |
| Average Age | 37 Years |
| Average Salary | 6.28K |
| Average Years at Company | 6.86 Years |

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

- Attrition by Education Field
- Attrition by Age Group
- Department-wise Job Role Matrix
- Gender Distribution
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Attrition by Job Role

## 💡 Key Insights

- Employees aged **26–35 years** have the highest attrition.
- **Life Sciences** contributes the largest percentage of employee attrition.
- Lower salary slabs experience higher employee turnover.
- Research Scientists and Laboratory Technicians show comparatively high attrition.

# 📈 Monthly Sales Growth Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This dashboard provides an overview of sales performance, profit analysis, and regional growth. It enables businesses to monitor KPIs and compare performance across different regions and products.

## ✨ Features

- Sales Overview
- Profit Analysis
- Region Filter
- Year Filter
- Product-wise Sales Analysis
- Interactive KPI Cards

## 📊 Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|----------|----------|
| Total Sales | 20M |
| Total Profit | 5M |
| Average Profit Margin | 25.09% |

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

- Monthly Sales Growth
- Profit by Region
- Sales by Product
- KPI Summary Cards
- Region-wise Comparison

## 💡 Key Insights

- North region generates the highest profit.
- Printers contribute the highest sales among products.
- Interactive filters allow year-wise and region-wise performance analysis.
- KPI cards provide a quick overview of business performance.
